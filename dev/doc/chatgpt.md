# ChatGPT

Here are notes about ChatGPT requests.

## Strengths

ChatGPT has several weaknesses as a writer.
* It is strong at representing a conventional point of view with conventional language.
* It has a diverse vocabulary and is eloquent and expressive.
* It can take a sketch of writing and expand it by filling in related concepts, which would normally require research.

## Weaknesses

ChatGPT has several weaknesses as a writer.
* It tends to reject original thinking in favor of reciting conventional thinking, so the insights of original statements are lost.
* It tends to reject critical thinking in favor of positive thinking, so straightforward negative statements are lost are rejected and replaced with positive or equivocal statements.
* It tends to use sophisticated vocabulary instead of clear vocabulary.
* When asked to simplify, it often throws out important details or uses generic terms instead of correct technical terms.
